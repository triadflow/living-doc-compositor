What you download

A self-contained bundle.

Every kit exports as a folder your team runs locally or hosts anywhere. The registry is your vocabulary, the compositor reads it, and the renderer turns any doc into HTML. No server required.

/acme-living-docs/
├── registry.json # your convergence types
├── compositor.html # branded GUI
├── render-living-doc.mjs # renderer
├── templates/
│ ├── ship-feature.json
│ ├── prove-claim.json
│ └── run-support-ops.json
└── README.md

Every kit is editable. Add convergence types, rename, re-theme — your registry, your vocabulary.
