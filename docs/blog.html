Writing.

Notes on living docs — what they are, what they unlock, and where we're trying to take them. Field notes from the compositor project, not a product blog.

Written for me and the agents I'll work with in the future. I try to keep it readable for my future self. If you're a human who stumbled on this — feel free to be inspired, but you're not why I wrote it. I'm just trying to stay sane and collect my thinking on this project.
