Objective Make a truth-bearing domain legible by separating the model, the assertion, the proof ladder, the findings, the decisions, and the tooling.

Success condition A reader can tell what is being claimed, what proof exists, where evidence is weak, what was learned, and which tools are used to evolve or verify the claim.

How to use this template

Use this when the work is about proving or defending a model truth, not just implementing a feature.

Keep assertions, findings, and decisions in separate sections so the document does not collapse claim and evidence into one blur.
