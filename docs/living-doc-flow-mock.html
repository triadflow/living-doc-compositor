The two arrows

Coverage (gray) is a completeness check — is every facet of the objective still carried by at least one section?

Invariant (amber) is the governance arrow — contracts the doc must keep following. Example: "decisions migrate out of verification." Scoped via appliesTo.

How to use this mock

Drag a facet's right port to a section's left port → coverage edge.
Drag an invariant's left port to a section's right port → governance arrow.
Click any wire to remove it. Click a node to focus its connections. Click "Doc-wide" on an invariant to toggle appliesTo: ["*"].
