How to use this mock

1. Drag from any facet's right port across to a section's left port to add a coverage edge.
2. Click a wire to remove it.
3. Click a node to highlight its connections — click the background to deselect.
4. Remove a facet or section with the × that appears on hover. Coverage edges follow.

Invariants

Scoped to one or more sections. They'd render as stickies attached to those sections in the real build; shown here as a sidebar list.

Export
